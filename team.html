Back to Work Movement

Cura takes pride in promoting women empowerment over the years. It has always believed in the power of women to bring in success for themselves and the organisation because of their unique strengths. Some of these strengths are good communication skills, empathy, inclusiveness, efficient productivity, and a knack for building strong relationships. Cura has been running this movement called “Back to work” for women who have been on a career break for varied reasons and are longing to get back to work. This movement has been a huge success with many such women working with Cura in the past and even today and doing exceptionally well for themselves and the Company!!!! They have regained their lost confidence and are raring to fight and take on any challenges in their professional or personal lives.
Dipty has been in the forefront behind this movement and truly believes that women can do wonders in their professional as well as personal lives given the right opportunity and flexibility. She is empathetic towards the need of women to balance their responsibilities on various fronts and helps them achieve all their goals. Today, Cura has many women who are doing exceptionally well in the field of real estate which traditionally has been male centric. All these women have come a long way to become what they are today. Under the able leadership of Dipty, Cura is proud to have maximum women representation in an industry which traditionally is disorganized and male dominated. “When the world tells you to shrink, expand.”
